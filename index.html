Who We Are

A Property Agency Built for Kisii

Enzi Mpya Homes is a locally owned and operated real estate agency based along Cathedral Road in Kisii Town. We help individuals, families, and businesses find, rent, buy, and sell property across Kisii County and the wider Nyanza region.

We are not a large corporate agency. We are a small, hands-on team with deep knowledge of the Kisii property market. We know which estates have water, which roads flood in April, which landlords maintain their properties, and which areas are growing in value. That local knowledge is what sets us apart.

Whether you are looking for a bedsitter near Kisii University, a shop along a busy road, a plot to build on, or a house to buy as an investment — we will help you find the right property at a fair price, without wasting your time.
